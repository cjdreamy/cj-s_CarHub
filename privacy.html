Privacy Notice & Data Protection

Effective Date: January 2026

Organization: Cj's_CarHub Kenya Online Marketplace

1. Introduction

Cj's_CarHub Kenya ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information in accordance with Kenyan data protection laws and ethical standards.

2. Legal Framework

We operate in compliance with the following Kenyan laws and policies:

  • Data Protection Act 2019: This Act establishes the framework for data protection in Kenya. We comply with the principles of lawfulness, fairness, transparency, purpose limitation, and security.
  • Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act 2018: We ensure your data is protected against unauthorized access, disclosure, and cyber threats.
  • National ICT Policy 2019: We align with national standards for digital services and cybersecurity.
  • Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) Guidelines: We follow the official guidelines issued by Kenya's ODPC.

3. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Registration Data: Full name, email, phone number, ID number, location (collected during registration)
  • Usage Data: Browsing history, search queries, car listings viewed, clicks, and interaction patterns
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device type, cookies, and timestamps
  • Communication Data: Messages between buyers and sellers (stored with consent)

4. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for:

  • Account creation and management
  • Providing marketplace services (listings, search, filtering)
  • User identification through secure cookies
  • Fraud prevention and platform security
  • Improving user experience and platform features
  • Sending notifications and updates (only with consent)
  • Complying with legal obligations

5. Cookies & User Identification

Cookies Used: We use secure, HttpOnly cookies to identify registered users and maintain their session. These cookies do NOT contain sensitive personal information and expire after 30 days of inactivity.

Cookie Purpose: These cookies allow us to personalize your experience, remember your preferences, and protect against unauthorized access.

Your Control: You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but this may affect your experience on our platform.

6. Data Security & Ethical Considerations

  • Encryption: Sensitive data (passwords) are never stored in plain text. User passwords are hashed using secure algorithms.
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel can access your data.
  • Data Minimization: We collect only the information necessary to provide our services.
  • Purpose Limitation: Your data is used only for stated purposes. We do NOT sell your data to third parties.
  • User Consent: We obtain explicit consent before collecting sensitive data or sending communications.

7. Your Rights Under Kenya's Data Protection Act 2019

As a user, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request access to your personal data at any time.
  • Right to Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Erasure: You can request deletion of your data (subject to legal retention requirements).
  • Right to Object: You can object to processing of your data for specific purposes.
  • Data Portability: You can request your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

8. Transparency & Accountability

  • We maintain records of data processing activities.
  • We conduct regular security audits and assessments.
  • In case of a data breach, we will notify affected users within 72 hours as required by law.
  • We cooperate fully with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC).

9. Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT share your personal data with third parties except:

  • With buyers/sellers (only information necessary for transactions)
  • When required by law or court order
  • With service providers who assist us (under strict confidentiality agreements)

10. Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained for the duration of your account.
  • Deleted Accounts: Data is securely deleted within 30 days, except where legal retention is required.
  • Transaction Records: Retained for 5 years for legal compliance.

11. Contact & Complaints

If you have questions about our privacy practices or wish to exercise your rights:

  • Email: privacy@Cj's_CarHub.com
  • Write to: Cj's_CarHub Kenya, Nairobi, Kenya
  • ODPC Complaints: You can lodge complaints with the Office of the Data Protection Commissioner (ODPC) at www.odpc.go.ke

12. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this privacy policy. Changes will be communicated to users via email or notice on our website. Continued use of Cj's_CarHub after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
